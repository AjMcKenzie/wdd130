Board Games

This is the page for board games and other tabletop games reviews. Here you can read about my reviews for different board games. I will have a pro and con table, with my pros and cons for each game. This page will have a heading with the game name and my reviews for each of them.





Unmacthed

    The point of this game is to control your fighter and verse your friends and their fighter. This is a strategy game where you have to use your actions to defeat the other fighter. The cool thing about this game is that it can be played very competitively or casually. This game can take a long time to play. It also would need at least two people to play. It has boards that can support up to four people. This game can be played in teams or a free-for-all.
    The cool thing about this game is that there are a lot of different fighters to choose from. Some of the fighters that you can player are: Sherlock Homes, Dr. Jackal & Mr. Hyde, Beowulf, Little Red Riding Hood. They also have some licensed characters like the Raptors and Ingen from Jurassic Park they also have characters like Moon Knight and Daredevil. These are only a few characters you can see the full list of them here.


Pros

  • Has a lot of cool characters to paly as
  • Can be a competitive or casual game
  • It's a different game each time, beacuse of the draw
  • Characters are well balanced

Cons

  • Can be a long game
  • The characters aren't sold separately
  • Needs at least two people
  • Can get expensive if you want all the characters
